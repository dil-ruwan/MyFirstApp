Welcome to Shirohana

Pioneering the art of designing the perfect floral settings to captivate and awaken a thousand emotions, Shirohana caters to the varied floral requirements of today’s market, sourced fresh from our very own farms

Constantly challenging boundaries and concepts, Shirohana is the leader in Sri Lanka’s cut-flower industry since its inception in 1983. Renowned for recognizing the power of flowers to awake a range of emotions, our highly acclaimed chain of flower shops can be found around the island - in Colombo, Negombo and Kandy. As the most reputable chain of flower shops in Sri Lanka, it has been our signature individuality and distinction in quality that has driven our success over the years, emerging as the ultimate flower shop experience, par none. Using only the finest flowers and foliage that captures the essence of each passing season, we offer customers a variety of bespoke bouquets, helping you deliver the message you want, whether it’s a gesture of love, luck or a simple thank you. Our designs inspire with its bright vibrant hues, reflecting its natural beauty while exuding sophistication, elegance and charm. The greatest advantage of Shirohana is that we grow our own flowers, which enables us to offer customers a diverse variety of fresh flowers at very competitive prices. Recognized for our generosity in hand-crafting every floral creation we do, our aim is offer custom-made floral arrangements based on your needs - whether it’s a fashionable flower arrangement or a romantic, intimate bouquet you require. Each arrangement is delivered with personalized cards for your own individual message.
